Written by Penny Juice on April 21, 2023

Nothing is tastier on a hot, summer day than an ice-cold popsicle. And with delicious and nutritious Penny Juice flavors, you and your kids can enjoy this fabulous dessert without the guilt. These frozen treats are easy to make, and can be customized with your favorite flavors and fruits. We'll share a simple recipe for a delicious and nutritious juice popsicle.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Penny Juice fruit juice (such as citrus punch, pineapple orange, or white grape)
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh fruit (such as strawberries, kiwi, or blueberries)
  • Popsicle molds
  • Popsicle sticks

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the fresh fruit juice and honey until well combined. The honey will add sweetness to the juice and also help the popsicles freeze more evenly.
  2. Add the chopped fresh fruit to the juice mixture and stir gently to distribute the fruit evenly. You can use any fruit you like, but we recommend using a mix of different fruits for a more complex flavor.
  3. Pour the juice mixture into the popsicle molds, leaving a little bit of space at the top to allow for expansion as the popsicles freeze.
  4. Insert the popsicle sticks into the molds, making sure they are centered in the middle of each popsicle.
  5. Place the molds in the freezer and let the popsicles freeze for at least 4 hours, or until they are completely frozen.
  6. To remove the popsicles from the molds, run the molds under warm water for a few seconds to loosen the popsicles.
  7. Serve the popsicles immediately, or store them in the freezer for up to 1 week.

Tips:

  • You can use any type of fresh fruit juice you like for this recipe, but we recommend using a juice that is not too thick or pulpy.
  • If you prefer your popsicles to be less sweet, you can reduce the amount of honey in the recipe or omit it altogether.
  • If you don't have popsicle molds, you can use small paper cups instead. Simply fill the cups with the juice mixture, cover them with foil, and insert the popsicle sticks through the foil.
  • Do you have another tip you'd like to share? Leave us a comment and we'll add it to the list!

Making your own juice popsicles is a fun and easy way to enjoy a healthy and delicious frozen treat. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create your own custom flavor combinations and enjoy them all summer long. Give this recipe a try and let us know what you (and your kids) think!
