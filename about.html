About Us

Penny Juice of America

Founded in 2001, Penny Juice is a trusted provider of high-quality juice products for childcare centers across the country. We understand that nutrition is crucial for children's healthy growth and development, which is why we are committed to providing the best possible juice products to our customers.

At Penny Juice, we believe that every child deserves access to delicious and nutritious juice that is made from 100% fruit juice concentrate with no added sugar or preservatives. Our juice products are carefully crafted to meet the needs of growing children, and we are proud to offer a range of flavors that children love.

Our mission is to make it easy for childcare centers to provide healthy and tasty juice options to their young charges. With over 40 years of experience in the beverage industry, we are proud to bring our passion to your children's tables and support the health and well-being of the next generation.

About Our Products

You won't find anything but real fruit in our products. Our 100% blended fruit juice concentrates meet the USDA and FDA guidelines regarding subsidy and nutrition. We've designed our juices specifically to meet the needs of children in day care, preschool, childcare, and basp.

Our juice concentrates are available in 15 flavors, including 7 color-free flavors. Each case contains six half-gallon bottles of your choice. Just mix one part concentrate to seven parts water and your children will enjoy a nutritious and delicious drink. With no need to refrigerate these products and our 1-gallon premeasured mix pitchers (sold separately), storage and preparation is simple.

Order today to give your kids great flavors they will love! We offer free shipping nationwide and no contracts. (minimum order: 1 case).
